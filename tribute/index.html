Bill Gates

Co-founder of Microsoft and Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

Here's a time line of Bill Gates's life:

  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, Washington
  • 1973 - Enrolled at Harvard College
  • 1975 - Left Harvard and founded Microsoft in Albuquerque, New Mexico with Paul Allen
  • 1985 - Launched their first retail version of Microsoft Windows
  • 1994 - Married Melinda French on the Hawaiian Island of Lanai
  • 2000 - Founded Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation in Seattle, Washington
  • 2015 - Wrote about the challenge of transitioning the world's energy system from one based primarily on fossil fuels to one based on sustainable energy sources
  • 2023 - An estimated net worth of US$133 billion, making him the fifth-richest person in the world according to Bloomberg Billionaires Index
  • 2024 - Bill Gates appreciated Indians for their innovation and shared a post of having tea made by internet sensation, Dolly Chaiwala. “In India, you can find innovation everywhere you turn—even in the preparation of a simple cup of tea!” he captioned the video while sharing on Instagram.

"First the machines will do a lot of jobs for us and not be super intelligent. That should be positive if we manage it well. A few decades after that though the intelligence is strong enough to be a concern. I agree with Elon Musk and some others on this and don't understand why some people are not concerned."

-- Forbes
